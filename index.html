BitcoinConf El Salvador

This year has shown us how acutely the fate of all nations are intertwined and how resilient a global crypto economy can be.

Speakers

Roger Ver

Roger is the CEO and founder of MemoryDealers.com. With an educational background in economics and computer science, Bitcoin caught his attention in late 2010 and he has been a devoted promoter of Bitcoin and the benefits of Digital Currencies as a technological advance that can greatly enhance the free flow of payments around the world.

Charlie Shrem

Mr. Shrem is a Bitcoin Pioneer, CTO of Intellisys.ai / Mainstreet.ky, & founder of the Bitcoin Foundation (Vice-Chairman 2012- 2014) As CEO of BitInstant, one of the earliest and largest Bitcoin companies processing 30% of all Bitcoin/USD volume, Mr. Shrem had developed joint ventures between traditional finance companies and Bitcoin companies such as Payza.com.

Maja Vujinovic

Maja Vujinovic started her career in mobile payments in Sub Saharan Africa. Working across 5 continents Maja built government and enterprise infrastructure across industries such as mobile payments, energy and healthcare. She took interest in bitcoin in 2011 and was instrumental in development of a first blockchain company in 2013. Maja currently runs OGroup.

Playa El Zonte (Bitcoin Beach)


El Zonte beach is located at kilometer 53 of the Litoral highway, in the Chiltiupán municipality of the department of La Libertad. Where you will find landscapes with a bohemian atmosphere. It is not surprising that El Zonte beach is considered a highly relevant tourist spot.

This beach has many places that can be of great interest to a good number of tourists. There are many reasons to go sightseeing at El Zonte beach. Many people have expressed how much fun they have had in visiting that beach. Photos cannot fully capture the beauty of your landscapes. Which you can only do when visiting their spaces, and your view will appreciate it.
